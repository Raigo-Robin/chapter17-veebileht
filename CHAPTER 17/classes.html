Classes

Welcome to Yoko's Kitchen, where we offer authentic Japanese cooking classes for anyone interested in learning the art of Japanese cuisine. Our classes are designed to teach you the fundamentals of Japanese cooking while also providing an immersive cultural experience.

Our classes are taught by expert chefs who have years of experience in the culinary arts. They will guide you through each step of the cooking process, sharing their knowledge and expertise to help you create delicious and authentic Japanese dishes.

We offer a variety of classes that cater to all skill levels, from beginner to advanced. Our classes cover a range of topics, including sushi making, ramen noodle soups, tempura, bento boxes, and more. Each class includes hands-on instruction, so you can learn by doing and get feedback from our expert instructors.

Our classes are held in a state-of-the-art kitchen that is fully equipped with everything you need to create amazing Japanese dishes. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to ensure that every dish is of the highest quality and bursting with flavor.

Our classes are perfect for individuals, couples, or groups of friends looking for a fun and unique experience. Whether you are a food enthusiast looking to expand your culinary skills or simply want to learn more about Japanese culture, our classes offer something for everyone. For example:

Sauce Masterclass

Public Class

An intensive one-day course looking at how to create the most delicious sauces for use in a range of Japanese cookery.

We also offer private classes for those looking for a more personalized experience. Our private classes are perfect for corporate team building events, birthdays, or any other special occasion. We can tailor our classes to meet your specific needs, so you can get the most out of your experience.

Japanese Vegetarian

Private Class

A five week introdution to traditional Japanese vegetarina meals, teaching you a selection of rice and noodle dishes.
